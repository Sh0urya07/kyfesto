The space

Murals, plants & good light.

Kyfesto is full of character — bold wall art, hanging plants, warm light and seating you'll actually want to linger in. Indoor when it's hot, open-air when the evening's nice.

It's the kind of place that's easy to love: chill enough to work, pretty enough for a date, fun enough to bring the whole gang.
